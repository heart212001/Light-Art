Black & White Photography

How to photograph black and white

  • Composition

    1

    Composition is a set of elements arranged together harmoniously and attractively, the basic elements in a composition are line, shape, texture, color tones, balance, pattern, movement, etc. In order to achieve attractive black and white images, the photographer must use the elements of composition in the best possible way to maintain the attention of the viewers and guide them helping them to realize the beauty, meaning and message of the image.

  • Block out Color

    2

    There are two ways to make black and white photos: The first way is to choose photos that can be converted from color photos to black and white photos using photo editing software. The second method is black and white photography with the camera. Composition elements such as the effect of light, shadow, texture, and others are used to determine the appropriate composition that is suitable for black and white photography. This method relies on the photographer's ability to train a sample to actually notice the best scenes and convert them .

  • Camera Settings

    3

    For black and white photography, the camera settings can be adjusted to shoot monochrome, in addition to shooting in the Raw format, in order to facilitate work on the colors that are used in image editing programs after taking pictures, and then display and evaluate the result on a computer screen or any LCD screen

  • Types of black& white photography

    4

    Black and white photography has a long history in the world of photography. It began before the appearance of colors and in all fields, as in black and white photographs and films, as well as in the world of journalism in newspapers and magazines.

    2-Streets
