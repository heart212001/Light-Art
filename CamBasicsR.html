Basics And Rules

  • 01

    1- Choose the right camera

    The photographer must determine the type of photography he is inclined to, determine the budget available to him first, and then buy the appropriate camera and lens

  • 02

    2- Study the description of the camera

    After purchasing the camera, you must read the description attached to it, and also watch the reviews of websites for the camera such as YouTube clips that describe the camera and a specific description so that the photographer is aware of the advantages and disadvantages of the camera

  • 03

    3- Find the necessary accessories

    But now, after buying the camera and learning about it and knowing its advantages and disadvantages, some types of photography must provide some tools that help in taking a better picture, some types of photography, such as photographing foods, there must be additional lighting, backgrounds and others

  • 04

    4- Practice using the camera

    Half of the professionalism is in a lot of practice, not in taking courses or learning in schools, but visual feeding and continuing to practice
